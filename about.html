Two-Way Patch

If you are here, you have probably been to Two-Way Patch at CodeBase (Edinburgh). How did The Common Sense by Melanie Gilligan make you feel?

The Common Sense presents a near future where invasive digital technology is no longer an option, but spread throughout society. It affects how we communicate, our relationships and our work-life balance. Did this future make you angry? Or are you anxioustly awaiting your own two-way patch?

The Common Sense is the first acquisition of the new Research Collection of the University of Edinburgh. Two-Way Patch will be on show at CodeBase from 29 March until 27 April.

To find out more about Two-Way Patch, The Common Sense and the full programme of events, please see the exhibition website.
