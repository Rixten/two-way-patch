Two-Way Patch

The Edinburgh College of Art Collections & Curating Programme at the University of Edinburgh is pleased to present Two-Way Patch: an installation of the University’s recently acquired The Common Sense by Melanie Giligan. The exhibition features a multi-channel video work on display between 29 March and 27 April at CodeBase. In conjunction with the Edinburgh Science Festival, this exhibition and Gilligan’s video work deal with themes of digital technology, privacy, work-life balance, and inter-personal relationships.

To find out more about Two-Way Patch, The Common Sense and the full programme of events, please see the exhibition website.
